# Target Journal Selection: npj Digital Medicine

## Selected journal

npj Digital Medicine (Nature Portfolio)

Journal page: https://www.nature.com/npjdigitalmed/

## Why this journal fits SentinelCare

1. Scope alignment:
   - digital health systems
   - AI/CDS in clinical workflow
   - implementation and validation studies
2. Audience alignment:
   - clinical informatics
   - digital medicine researchers
   - translational deployment teams
3. Article type fit:
   - "Article" category accepts substantial primary work and systematic/scoping evidence syntheses relevant to digital medicine.

## Key formatting requirements used for this package

Primary source:  
https://www.nature.com/npjdigitalmed/for-authors-and-referees/about/content-types

For "Article":

1. Title page:
   - title up to 15 words
   - full authors and affiliations
   - corresponding email
2. Abstract:
   - no subheadings
   - up to 150 words
3. Required sections:
   - Introduction
   - Results
   - Discussion
   - Methods
   - Data Availability (mandatory)
   - Code Availability (where applicable)
   - Acknowledgements
   - Author contributions
   - Competing interests
4. References:
   - limited to 60 (not strictly enforced)

Submission guidance source:  
https://www.nature.com/npjdigitalmed/for-authors-and-referees/submission-guidelines

Notable requirements captured:

1. Cover letter required.
2. Reporting summary/editorial checklist needed at peer review stage; encouraged at submission.
3. Data availability statement required.
