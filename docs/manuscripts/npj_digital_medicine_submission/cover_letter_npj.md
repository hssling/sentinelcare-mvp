Subject: Submission of original Article manuscript - "SentinelCare: Multidomain Patient Safety Intelligence for Real-Time Clinical Risk Control"

Dear Editor,

Please find attached our manuscript entitled "SentinelCare: Multidomain Patient Safety Intelligence for Real-Time Clinical Risk Control" for consideration as an Article in npj Digital Medicine.

This manuscript presents the conceptual and translational implementation framework of SentinelCare, a modular patient-safety intelligence platform designed for real-time risk detection, accountable escalation, and closed-loop workflow support across medication safety, critical-result follow-up, and deterioration surveillance domains.

The work is relevant to npj Digital Medicine readership because it addresses practical deployment barriers at the intersection of digital health engineering, clinical workflow integration, and governance-first safety implementation. The manuscript emphasizes:

1. multidomain safety system design rather than single-model prediction,
2. explicit human-in-the-loop controls,
3. queue/escalation operationalization,
4. policy and validation artifact generation to support responsible deployment.

To our knowledge, this integrated architecture and staged validation framing provide a useful translational template for healthcare organizations seeking to implement real-time safety intelligence under evolving regulatory and quality expectations.

This manuscript is original, has not been published previously, and is not under consideration elsewhere. All authors have approved submission.
