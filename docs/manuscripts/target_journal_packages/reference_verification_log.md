## Purpose

This log records the final reference-verification pass applied across the `JAMIA Open` submission packages for Manuscripts A, B, and C.

## Verification method

1. Journal-instruction fit was rechecked against the current `JAMIA Open` author guidance.
2. Clinical and informatics literature references were checked against PubMed or official publisher pages where applicable.
3. Policy and regulatory references were checked against official organizational sources.
4. Citation formatting was normalized to a consistent Vancouver-style house format across the manuscript series:
   - author list
   - title sentence case
   - journal title abbreviation where standard
   - year, volume, issue, and page or article identifier where available
   - organizational author and place/publisher structure for policy documents
