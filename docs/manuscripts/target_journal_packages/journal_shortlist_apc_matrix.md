# APC Journal Shortlist for SentinelCare Framework Manuscripts

## Shortlist

### 1. JAMIA Open

- Publisher: Oxford University Press / AMIA
- Fit: strong for clinical informatics, digital workflow systems, formulation and formative evaluation of new informatics models
- Article type fit: `Research and Applications`
- Official author guidance:
  - structured abstract with headings `Objectives, Materials and Methods, Results, Discussion, Conclusion`
  - main text should include `Background and Significance` plus corresponding sections
  - word count up to 4,000
  - tables up to 4
  - figures up to 6
- APC: `$3,950`
- Sources:
  - https://academic.oup.com/jamiaopen/pages/general_instructions

### 2. Frontiers in Digital Health

- Publisher: Frontiers
- Fit: strong for digital health systems, policy/practice review, human factors, health informatics
- Article type fit:
  - `Original Research`
  - `Systematic Review`
  - `Review`
  - `Policy & Practice Review`
- Official author guidance:
  - Original Research max 12,000 words with `Abstract, Introduction, Materials and Methods, Results, Discussion`
  - Policy & Practice Review max 12,000 words
  - minimum 5 and maximum 8 keywords
- APC:
  - A-type article `CHF 3,150`
- Sources:
  - https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/digital-health/sections/connected-health/for-authors/publishing-fees
  - https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/digital-health/sections/human-factors-and-digital-health/for-authors/article-types
  - https://www.frontiersin.org/guidelines

### 3. npj Digital Medicine

- Publisher: Nature Portfolio
- Fit: excellent prestige and scope, but best suited when manuscript presents stronger empirical primary research package
- Article type fit: `Article`
- Official author guidance:
  - title up to 15 words
  - abstract up to 150 words, no subheadings
  - sections include `Introduction, Results, Discussion, Methods`
- APC:
  - Original Research `£3090 / $4290 / EUR3390`
- Sources:
  - https://www.nature.com/npjdigitalmed/open-access
  - https://www.nature.com/npjdigitalmed/journal-information

## Choice

Selected target for current Manuscript A package: `JAMIA Open`

## Why JAMIA Open is the best immediate choice

1. The article type explicitly allows the formulation and formative evaluation of a new model.
2. The manuscript can honestly combine:
   - blueprint-derived framework design,
   - literature-guided rationale,
   - formative prototype outputs from the current SentinelCare implementation.
3. The structured format is strict and publication-oriented without forcing a narrower primary clinical trial frame prematurely.
4. The audience is highly aligned with clinical informatics, CDS, health IT implementation, and patient safety operations.
