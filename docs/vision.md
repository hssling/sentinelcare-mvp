# Vision

## Problem

Clinical harm often emerges from delayed follow-up, missed actions, and fragmented safety signals across medication workflows, diagnostics, and deterioration detection.

## SentinelCare Position

SentinelCare is an operational safety layer, not an autonomous clinician.
It computes risk and process deviations early, routes evidence-backed interventions, and captures full audit history with human override.

## Product principles

1. Human-in-the-loop for high-risk decisions.
2. High precision before interruption.
3. Workflow-native corrective actions.
4. Explainability and auditability by default.
5. Governance-first deployment with staged validation.

## Initial focus

1. Medication safety.
2. Critical-result closure.
3. Deterioration surveillance.
