# Governance and Operating Model

## Governance tiers

1. facility patient safety committee,
2. district surveillance review cell,
3. state patient safety intelligence cell,
4. national steering and scientific advisory group.

## Governance functions

1. taxonomy stewardship,
2. release approval for scoring and signal rules,
3. sentinel event escalation review,
4. publication and public reporting oversight,
5. privacy and ethical review,
6. state and national benchmarking governance.

## Role model

1. reporter,
2. facility safety officer,
3. investigator,
4. district reviewer,
5. state epidemiology and quality lead,
6. national analyst,
7. governance approver,
8. public reporting administrator.

## Release and rule governance

1. draft,
2. under review,
3. approved pilot,
4. approved production,
5. superseded,
6. retired.
