# Advocacy Memo

## Audience

Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, National Health Authority, National Health Mission, state health departments, teaching hospitals, and quality accrediting bodies.

## Core argument

Patient safety surveillance should be treated as essential public-health and health-system intelligence infrastructure, not only as an accreditation or hospital quality activity.

## Messages

1. patient safety events create avoidable mortality, disability, cost, and loss of trust,
2. India needs a structured, non-punitive surveillance and learning system,
3. the platform should support both low-digital and high-digital settings,
4. ABDM-era infrastructure makes phased integration feasible,
5. governance and privacy must be built in from the start.
