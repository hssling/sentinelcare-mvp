# Legal and Regulatory Alignment

## Alignment themes

1. health data privacy and consent architecture,
2. patient confidentiality and medico-legal sensitivity,
3. non-punitive protected reporting principles,
4. interoperability with national digital health infrastructure,
5. alignment with hospital quality and accreditation norms.

## Design stance

The platform should support protected quality-improvement reporting, role-based access, and de-identified analytic workflows while preserving traceability for authorized investigations.
