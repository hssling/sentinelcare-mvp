# Cover letter

To  
The Editor  
Indian Journal of Medical Research

Subject: Submission of Special Article titled `Governance, accountability, and bounded AI in patient safety surveillance: an implementation framework for India`

Dear Editor,

Please consider our manuscript for publication as a Special Article in the Indian Journal of Medical Research. The paper synthesizes governance, accountability, audit, and AI-safety requirements for patient safety surveillance systems and interprets their relevance for India.

The manuscript fits IJMR because it addresses public health, digital health governance, health-system implementation, and patient safety. It is framed as a policy- and systems-oriented special article and does not overstate implementation effectiveness where prospective evaluation remains pending.

This manuscript is original, is not under consideration elsewhere, and has been prepared for IJMR-aligned submission.
