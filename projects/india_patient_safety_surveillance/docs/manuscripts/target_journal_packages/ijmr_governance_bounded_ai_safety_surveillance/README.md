# IJMR Governance and AI Package

This folder contains an IJMR-compatible manuscript package focused on governance, accountability, and bounded AI use in patient safety surveillance systems.

## Proposed article type

- `Special Article`

## Manuscript focus

This paper addresses:

1. governance requirements for patient safety surveillance
2. accountability and audit design in multi-level reporting systems
3. safe and bounded use of AI assistance in safety surveillance workflows
