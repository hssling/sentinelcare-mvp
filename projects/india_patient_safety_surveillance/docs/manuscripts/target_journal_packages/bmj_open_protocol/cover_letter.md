# Cover Letter

Editor
BMJ Open

Re: Submission of "Incidence, patterns, and preventability of medical errors and adverse events in India: a systematic review and meta-analysis protocol"

This manuscript is a protocol for a systematic review and meta-analysis intended to quantify the incidence, distribution, severity, and preventability of medical errors and adverse events in Indian healthcare settings. It is designed to support evidence-informed development of national patient safety surveillance and policy.

The protocol fits BMJ Open because it addresses health-services research, transparent pre-specified synthesis methods, and a question of national policy relevance.
