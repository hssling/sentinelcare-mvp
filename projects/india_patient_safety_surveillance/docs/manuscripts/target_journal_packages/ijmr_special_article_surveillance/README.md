# IJMR Submission Package

This folder contains an Indian Journal of Medical Research (`IJMR`) aligned package for a `Special Article` on the India Patient Safety Surveillance System.

## Target article type

- `Special Article`
- fit rationale: IJMR explicitly includes reviews on digital health and pertinent topics of current interest under `Special Articles`, and applies review article instructions to them

## Why this manuscript type was chosen

The current project does not yet have multicentric empirical outcome data strong enough for an honest IJMR original article. A review-style special article is the correct fit because it allows:

1. an evidence-based synthesis of patient safety reporting and learning system design
2. an India-focused digital public health and governance framing
3. a defensible conceptual and operational architecture for national implementation

## IJMR guidance verified

Current IJMR instructions checked from official sources:

- IJMR For Authors: https://ijmr.org.in/for-authors/
- IJMR author instructions PDF: https://ijmr.org.in/wp-content/uploads/sites/178/2025/03/IJMR-Author-Instructions.pdf

Key fit points from the official instructions:

- IJMR covers public health, epidemiology, community medicine, and innovative health technologies
- IJMR does not charge article processing fees
- Special Articles include digital health and related topics of current interest
- Review-style manuscripts may be up to about `4000` words excluding abstract and references
- Review-style manuscripts require a clear methodology enumerating the search strategy and an unstructured abstract of about `250` words
