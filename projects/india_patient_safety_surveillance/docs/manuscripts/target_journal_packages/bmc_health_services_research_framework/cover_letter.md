# Cover Letter

Editor
BMC Health Services Research

Re: Submission of "A federated national surveillance framework for medical errors and patient safety in India"

This manuscript presents a governance-first, interoperability-ready national surveillance framework for medical errors and patient safety in India. It is positioned as a health-systems design and implementation paper, not as a completed national rollout claim. The paper defines a federated facility-district-state-national model, minimum dataset, signal and investigation workflow, and integration pathway with India's evolving digital health infrastructure.

The manuscript fits BMC Health Services Research because it addresses health-system design, service improvement, quality and safety, implementation feasibility, and national policy relevance.

The work is original, has not been published previously, and is not under consideration elsewhere.
