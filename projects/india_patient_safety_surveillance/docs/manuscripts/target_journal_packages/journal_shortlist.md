# Journal Shortlist for India Patient Safety Surveillance Manuscript Series

## Selected targets

### 1. BMC Health Services Research

- Best fit: national surveillance framework and health-system architecture paper
- Why: strong alignment with health-systems design, implementation, quality, governance, and national service-delivery research
- Official sources:
  - https://bmchealthservres.biomedcentral.com/submission-guidelines/preparing-your-manuscript
  - https://www.biomedcentral.com/getpublished/article-processing-charges/biomedcentral-prices

### 2. Frontiers in Health Services

- Best fit: technical architecture and implementation strategy paper
- Why: suitable for implementation, service design, health-system innovation, and operations-focused digital infrastructure
- Official sources:
  - https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/health-services#for-authors
  - https://www.frontiersin.org/guidelines
  - https://www.frontiersin.org/about/publishing-fees

### 3. BMJ Open

- Best fit: systematic review and meta-analysis protocol
- Why: established protocol pathway, broad health-services readership, and compatibility with surveillance-focused evidence synthesis planning
- Official sources:
  - https://bmjopen.bmj.com/pages/authors/
  - https://authors.bmj.com/before-you-submit/article-processing-charges/
