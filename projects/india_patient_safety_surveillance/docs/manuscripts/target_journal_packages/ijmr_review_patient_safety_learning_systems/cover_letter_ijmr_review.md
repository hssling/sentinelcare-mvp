# Cover letter

To  
The Editor  
Indian Journal of Medical Research

Subject: Submission of Special Article titled `Patient safety reporting and learning systems: lessons from the literature and implications for India`

Dear Editor,

Please consider our manuscript for publication as a Special Article in the Indian Journal of Medical Research. This manuscript synthesizes the international literature on incident reporting and patient safety learning systems and interprets its relevance for Indian health-system design, governance, and implementation.

The manuscript is suitable for IJMR because it addresses patient safety, public health systems, health-services organization, and digital health implementation. It is intended as a review-based synthesis for policy, research, and programme design rather than as a report of completed multicentric intervention data.

This manuscript is original, is not under consideration elsewhere, and has been prepared for IJMR submission requirements. No human participants were enrolled and no primary patient-level dataset was analyzed.

Sincerely,

Dr Siddalingaiah H S  
Professor, Community Medicine  
Shridevi Institute of Medical Sciences and Research Hospital, Tumkur, India
