# Signal Detection and Investigation SOP

## Trigger categories

1. sentinel event,
2. repeated similar events in one facility,
3. district or state cluster,
4. medicine, device, blood, or procedure-associated pattern,
5. unexplained increase in harm severity.

## Investigation steps

1. verify data integrity,
2. assign lead reviewer,
3. collect source documents,
4. classify contributory factors,
5. assess preventability and system failure points,
6. assign corrective actions,
7. monitor closure and recurrence.
