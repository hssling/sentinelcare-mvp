# Facility Reporting SOP

## Objective

Standardize event detection, reporting, triage, and local action at the facility level.

## Minimum steps

1. detect and report event or near miss,
2. perform immediate patient safety action,
3. classify event using the standard taxonomy,
4. assign local safety owner,
5. submit record to state or district node within timeline,
6. complete root-cause or contributory-factor review where indicated,
7. document corrective and preventive action,
8. close and archive after review.

## Reporting timelines

1. sentinel event: within 24 hours,
2. severe harm event: within 48 hours,
3. moderate and low harm routine events: within 7 days,
4. near misses and thematic audit data: scheduled batch submission.
