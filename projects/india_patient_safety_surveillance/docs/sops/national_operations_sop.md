# National SOP

## Objective

Define the standard operating procedure for national coordination of patient safety surveillance.

## Workflow

1. receive aggregated state and sentinel facility feeds,
2. validate completeness and data quality,
3. monitor predefined severe-event and cluster rules,
4. trigger national review for sentinel patterns,
5. issue advisories, learning notes, and benchmark reports,
6. maintain taxonomy, data dictionary, and investigation standards.

## Outputs

1. monthly national surveillance summary,
2. quarterly thematic bulletins,
3. rapid alerts for severe multi-state hazards,
4. annual patient safety report.
