## Design implications

1. use a federated architecture compatible with national registries,
2. support low-digital and high-digital reporting modes,
3. separate public-health surveillance semantics from patient safety semantics while allowing bridge integration,
4. keep privacy, audit, and role-based review central,
5. support institutional learning and national benchmarking simultaneously.
